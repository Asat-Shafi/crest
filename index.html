What's there for Special Needs Students ?



Asia's 1st online platform conduct Olympiad exams for special needs students. CREST Olympiads has made suitable modifications in the curriculum & examination system to meet the needs of such students & helping them to give an exposure of competitive environment that exists. Students from Prep/Kg till grade 10 can appear.
